# Template: Sprint / Iteration Summary

**Use this template by saying something like:**
> "Write a sprint summary for Growth squad" or "Summarise what shipped this sprint"

---

## Agent Instructions

When using this template, produce a **markdown file** written to
`outputs/YYYY-MM/sprint-summary-[squad]-[sprint-id].md`.

### Audience
The squad itself, plus adjacent teams and leadership who want a quick read.
Tone: honest, concise, peer-level. Not a status report to management —
a useful record the team would actually want to read.

### Structure to follow

```markdown
# Sprint Summary — [Squad] — [Sprint ID or Date Range]

## What we shipped
- [Item]: [one-line description of outcome, not task]
- ...

## What we didn't finish (and why)
- [Item]: [honest reason — scope, blocked, deprioritised]

## Metrics moved (if applicable)
| Metric | Before | After |
|--------|--------|-------|
| ...    | ...    | ...   |

## Blockers and dependencies
- [Blocker]: [who owns resolution]

## Next sprint focus
- [Top 2–3 priorities going into next sprint]

## Notes / shoutouts
[Optional — anything worth calling out]
```

### What to read first

1. The roadmap file for the squad or current period
2. `context/org.md` — to get squad and ownership context right
